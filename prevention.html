Prevention After Kidney Transplant

1. Wear a mask for 3 months for 24 hours (except while washing your face and eating).

2. After 3 months, wear a mask when you go out of your room.

3. Avoid people near you.

4. Do not touch anyone.

5. Stay quarantined in a room for 6 months.

6. Walk on the roof/terrace after 3 months, morning and evening, for 40 minutes each time.

7. Do not eat stale food for lifetime.

8. Clean your surgical area with sanitizer and apply skin ointment or coconut oil with cotton or tissue paper.

9. Drink mineral-packed water bottles for 6 months.

10. After 6 months, boil RO water before drinking it.

11. Eat only what Doctors have prescribed and at the advised time. (In my case, chicken was allowed once in every 15 days in the first month,but after 2 months, they allowed me to eat it weekly).

12. Use clothes, bedsheets, and towels washed in Dettol liquid.

13. Ensure that,the room is neat and clean, and clean the floor with Dettol.

14. Always keep extra medicines for 3 days.

15. Bathe daily with Dettol soap for at least 6 months.

16. Do not lift heavy weight for lifetime..
