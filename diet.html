DIALYSIS & TRANSPLANT

Diet During Dialysis

Morning

7:00 AM: Tea (1 small cup) + Biscuits, soaked dry fruits
8:00-9:00 AM: Poha/Upma/Idli-Sambhar/Roti-Sabzi, boiled egg whites (4)/sewai/kheer/suji halwa/sabudana
11:00 AM: 1 fruit (Apple/Papaya/Pear/Pineapple/Guava) [100 grams only]

Lunch

1:00 PM: Dal (without chilka), rice, roti, sabzi (all green vegetables except leafy vegetables like coriander leaves, spinach). Eat non-veg one day before your dialysis.

Evening

4:00 PM: Snacks of your choice (water intake must be according to the doctor's advice).

Dinner

8:00 PM: Chicken/paneer, rice, roti, sabzi (all green vegetables except leafy vegetables like coriander leaves, spinach). Eat non-veg one day before your dialysis.
9:00 PM: Milk (1 small cup).

Foods/Drinks to Avoid

1. Do not drink coconut water (high potassium).
2. Do not eat dal with chilka.
3. Do not eat leafy green vegetables.
4. Avoid achar (pickle), papad.
5. Limit salt intake to maintain blood pressure (you can eat salty chips during dialysis).
6. Do not eat organ meats in non-veg.
7. Avoid soft drinks.

Diet After Transplant

Morning

6:00 AM: 1 glass lukewarm water, 1 cup tea/green tea with 2-4 millet biscuits/Marie Gold biscuits
7:30 AM: 2 boiled egg whites, 1 boiled fruit (Apple/Papaya), 1 cup turmeric milk, Moong dal chilla/uttapam/roti-sabzi.

Mid-Morning

10:30 AM: 2 roti with sattu/sewai/kheer/halwa/sabudana.

Lunch

1:30 PM: Dal (Moong/Masoor/Toor/Urad/Black/Chana), rice, roti, sabzi (1 paneer dish & 1 green vegetable), boiled salad (Carrot & Cucumber).

Evening

4:00 PM: Tea/green tea with 2-4 millet biscuits/Marie Gold biscuits, roasted makhana (1 small bowl).

Dinner

8:00 PM: Dal (Moong/Masoor/Toor/Urad/Black/Chana), rice, roti, sabzi (1 paneer dish & 1 green vegetable), boiled salad (Carrot & Cucumber).

Foods/Drinks to Avoid

1. Do not eat anything raw for at least 1 year after the transplant.
2. Do not drink tap water.

Foods/Drinks to Include

1. Eat non-veg as advised by the doctor.
2. Drink mineral water or boiled water.
