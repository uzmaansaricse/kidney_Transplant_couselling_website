Procedure (Pre-Transplant)

1. Do timely dialysis.
2. Try to eat less oily and spicy food before the transplant (10 days prior).
3. Start checkups for kidney donor and recipient.

Procedure (During Transplant)

1. Be admitted the day as doctor told you.
2. Stay calm and trust the doctors.
3. On the day of surgery: Do not eat or drink anything after dinner the previous night.
4. Doctors will administer injections in the backbone, neck, and artery line (hand).
5. You will be taken to the operation room, given anesthesia, and will feel nothing during surgery.
6. When you wake up, you will feel much better and proud of your decision.
7. Observe what they provide you to eat and how they maintain hygiene. It’s crucial to prevent kidney infection.
8. Due to immunosuppressant medicines, there is a high risk of infection.
9. Take good care of yourself.
10. After 20 days, stitches and the DJ stent (a small pipe in the urinary tract) will be removed, and you will be free from everything.

Procedure (Post-Transplant: First 3 Months)

1. Take medicines on time (set alarms).
2. Maintain hygiene.
3. Clean yourself daily with antibacterial wipes.
4. Change your clothes daily.
5. Drink water as per the doctor’s advice.
6. Follow a diet with very little oil and spices.
7. Wash vegetables with boiled water before cooking.
8. Change bed sheets daily.
9. Wear a belt as advised.
10. Measure urine input/output for the first week.
11. Sanitize your belongings regularly.
12. Keep your hands sanitized every 30 minutes or whenever you touch anything.
13. Wash your hands with Dettol handwash, then sanitize before eating.
14. Do not eat anything without brushing your teeth.
15. Walk around the room.
16. Stay quarantined in one room and avoid meeting outsiders.
17.Do not lift heavy weight.
18.Entertain Youself.Keep Happy Mind always.
19.Check Your Bp & Sugar Level Regular.

Procedure (Post-Transplant: Lifetime)

1. Take medicines on time (set alarms).
2. Maintain hygiene.
3. Do not eat outside food.
4. Drink only mineral water / boiled water.
5. Drink water as per the doctor’s advice.
6. Follow a healthy diet.
7. Maintain physical activity.
8. Wear mask whenever you go in crowd.
9. Maintain distance with infected people.
10. Do not take any medicine without asking your doctor.
11. Sanitize your belongings regularly.
12.Do not lift heavy weight.
13.Entertain Youself.Keep Happy Mind always.
